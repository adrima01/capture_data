Online payment of fines service

You can pay by credit card (pay or deposit) any fines where the e-payment number is present on the payment form, as well as the increased post-parking fees.

Important

Paying the fine implies you acknowledge the offence. If you wish to appeal the offence or designate another driver, do not pay and complete promptly the procedure online.
