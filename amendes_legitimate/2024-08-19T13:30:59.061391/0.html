Accessibility statement

Pursuant to article 47 of French law No. 2005-102 of 11 February 2005, the French Directorate General of Public Finances (DGFiP) undertakes to make its internet, intranet, extranet and software packages accessible to all (as well as its mobile applications and digital urban infrastructure).

To achieve this, the French Directorate General of Public Finances (DGFiP) has implemented the following strategies and actions:

  • A three-year accessibility plan 2020-2022
  • Actions completed 2020-2021

This accessibility statement applies to Amendes.

Compliance status

The Amendes website is only partially compliant with version 4 of the RGAA (French general accessibility improvement framework), due to the non-conformities and exemptions listed below.

Test results

The compliance audit carried out by the OPEN GROUP revealed that:

  • 93% of RGAA version 4 criteria are met;
  • The website's average compliance rate is 98%.

Non-accessible content

Non-compliance

  • Criterion 5.2 The summary is not always relevant for some complex data tables.
  • Criterion 5.5 The title is not always relevant for some complex data tables.
  • Criterion 10.2 On some web pages, visible content that shows information does not remain present when style sheets are disabled.
  • Criterion 13.3 On some web pages, certain downloadable office documents do not have an accessible version.

Derogations for disproportionate workload

  • None

Content not subject to accessibility requirements

  • Both pages of the fine payment website (reason: third-party service)

Issue date of this accessibility statement

This declaration was issued on 14 April 2023.

Improvement and contact

If you are unable to access any services or content, you can contact the French Directorate General of Public Finances (DGFiP), amendes.gouv.fr in order to request an accessible alternative or receive the content in a different format.

  • Send a message to accessibilite.amendes@dgfip.finances.gouv.fr
  • Contact the French Directorate General of Public Finances (DGFiP), Responsable de amendes.gouv.fr, 139 rue de Bercy, 75572 Paris, Cedex 12

How to appeal

If you find that a lack of accessibility prevents you from accessing any website content or functions, and you report this to us but do not receive a response, you have the right to file a complaint or a request for referral with the Défenseur des droits (Defender of Rights).

There are several ways to do this:

  • Write a message to the Défenseur des droits
  • Contact the Défenseur des droits delegate for your region
  • Send a letter (free of charge, no stamp required) to Défenseur des droits - Libre réponse 71120 - 75342 Paris CEDEX 07
