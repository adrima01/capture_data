Privacy / Personal information / Cookies and other trackers

amendes.gouv.fr website

Processing of personal data

All the personal data on the www.amendes.gouv.fr website are the subject of various treatments declared to the CNIL and implemented by the Direction générale des Finances publiques (DGFiP) in its capacity as controller.

These processings of personal data are for the purpose of :

  • the ability to pay a fine notice online;
  • the possibility of consulting a fine notice payment;

Right of modification

In accordance with the Law of 6 January 1978 amended relating to computer files and liberties, subjects can at any time access the information about them and modify the inaccurate data.

Cookies and other trackers

The website amendes.gouv.fr uses trackers.

A tracker is any operation of reading and/or writing information to a user's electronic communications terminal.
A cookie is a file stored by a server on a user's terminal (computer, telephone, etc.) and associated with a web domain (i.e. in most cases with all the pages of a single website). It is a special type of tracker.

The Directorate General of Public Finances only uses on this website trackers that are strictly necessary for the proper functioning of the site and which do not require your consent.

These trackers are described below :

Tracker nameFunctionTracker validity period
Cookie « TF01efc40a »Allows dialogue between the front application and the back servicesTime of user session
Cookie « PAYPAGE_SESSIONID »Allows the buyer's payment session to be foundTime of payment session
Cookie « TSxxxxxxxx »Allows requests to be securedTime of payment session
Cookie « JSESSIONID »Allows the buyer's payment session to be foundTime of payment session
Cookie « RESPCTX »Allows the browser to be redirected to the merchant site where the payment request originatedTime of payment session

You can block these cookies by changing your browser settings. However, please note that blocking cookies that are strictly necessary for the operation of the site may adversely affect your user experience. Indeed, some features of the site may no longer work.
