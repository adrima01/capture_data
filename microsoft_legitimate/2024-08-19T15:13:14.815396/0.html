    Overview

    Your AI assistant for work

    • Get up to speed in less time with cross-application intelligence working seamlessly across all your data.
    • Easily track discussions and suggested action items in real time.
    • Get helpful coaching tips to create impactful customer communications.
    • Partner with Copilot to write, edit, and summarize content. 
    • Transform written content into compelling multimedia pitch decks and presentations with a single command. 
    • Analyze and explore data to create assets such as powerful, professional visualizations and project trackers.
    • Copilot inherits the security, compliance, and privacy policies youve set up in Microsoft 365.
    • Your data never leaves its secure partition and is never used for training purposes.
    • Get accurate, relevant answers anchored in your business content and working context.
