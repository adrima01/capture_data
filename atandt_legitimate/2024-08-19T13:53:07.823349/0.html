    Find the perfect Samsung phone with AT&T

    There's a reason Samsung phones are some of the most popular in the world. The latest models are everything you are looking for in a piece of next-generation technologyquality cameras, sleek design, powerful hardware, and a great customer experience. Read our Samsung phone buying guide if need help choosing a new phone. We also have a Samsung phone comparison tool to help you compare features and specs.

    Why AT&T

    If you plan on using your phone on the move, your wireless network is going to make a massive difference in the quality of your experience. AT&T 5G is nationwide, includes the latest security technology built-in, and is included in all Unlimited plants at no extra cost1. So you can do more of what you love on your phone, whether its streaming music, downloading pictures, or catching up on your favorite shows.

    Which Samsung Phone?

    AT&T offers a wide range of new Samsung phones, from the hottest releases to more affordable options from previous generations. No matter what youre looking for, you should be able to find something that fits your needs and budget. 

    Tech lovers looking for the newest experience should consider the new Galaxy S24 , S24+ and S24 Ultra . The Samsung Galaxy S24 series is the most powerful Galaxy yet, with superior hardware updates like a powerful 50MP wide angle lens camera along with a bigger, brighter display for the S24 and S24+. The Galaxy S24 Ultra has a 200MP main rear camera capable of Space Zoom 100x. 

    If you like the retro vibe, the new Samsung Galaxy Z Flip6 and the Samsung Galaxy Z Fold6 may be more your speed. The Flip6 is great for budding filmmakers, with an improved 50MP camera with a camcorder mode featuring Smooth Zoom for Steadicam-like shots. If you prefer a bigger screen for surfing and streaming on the go, the Fold6 gives you exactly thatand you can multitask with Multi-active Window, which lets you open up to three windows at a time.  

    Each of the Galaxy phones are equipped with Galaxy AI, which powers features like Photo Assist, where with the help of generative AI you can remove unwanted objects and reflections from photos. Other features include Interpreter, which helps you talk with someone in up to 16 languages.

    Get your Samsung phone and accessories today

    AT&T makes it easier to get your next Samsung phone with free same-day delivery or in-store pickup. We also provide support for online activation, device setup, number transferring, and much more. Browse our selection of Samsung accessories and peripherals that are compatible with your phone like Samsung Galaxy Buds, Galaxy Watches, Galaxy Smart Rings, and phone cases.
