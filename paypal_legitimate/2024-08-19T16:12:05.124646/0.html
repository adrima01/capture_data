Frequently asked questions

To add cash to PayPal, go to your Wallet in the PayPal app while you're at the store. Then, select Add Cash. Select the store you're in, get a barcode, and show it to the cashier at checkout. Your money will be added to your balance, usually in minutes.

You don't need a bank account to use PayPal.

You can typically add any cash amount from $20.00 USD to $500.00 USD per barcode. You can use up to 3 barcodes or add up to $500.00 USD per day. The PayPal cash limit is $500.00 USD per day and a total of $4,000.00 USD per month.

You can add cash to PayPal at 90,000+ locations around the US. Some retailers include Rite Aid, CVS Pharmacy, GameStop, Walmart, Dollar General, and Family Dollar.

Money is typically added to your PayPal Balance account in minutes.
