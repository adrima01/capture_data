Frequently asked questions

PayPal is an online payment system that allows you to send and receive money around the world. You can link your bank account, credit card, or debit card. You can also have a balance in your PayPal account.

The PayPal app is one simple mobile app to:

  • Send and request money.
  • Keep track of your money and get cash back offers in the app.
  • Follow your packages on a map, get updates, and much more.

You can link 4 cards to your unverified an account with PayPal. When your account is verified, you can link up to 24 cards in your PayPal mobile wallet app.

You can download the PayPal app from the iOS App Store or Android Play Store.

We don’t charge any fees to open an account with PayPal or download the PayPal app. We do charge fees for the following:

  • When you receive payments from a purchase.
  • When you receive payments from outside your country or region.

For more information on fees, click Fees at the bottom of any PayPal page.
