With your business debit card, you have instant access to the money available in your PayPal Business account. As soon as new payments are deposited or transferred to your account, you can spend or withdraw it as cash using your PayPal Business Debit Card.

Your 1% cash back benefit is added to your PayPal balance weekly as qualifying transactions settle. That’s more money to spend or reinvest in your business.

In addition to earning 1% cash back on eligible purchases,2 you can now earn bonus rebates at 50,000+ businesses with Mastercard Easy Savings️ when shopping with your PayPal Business Debit Mastercard.4 This money is added to your PayPal balance automatically as qualifying transactions settle.
