How does PayPal work FAQs

PayPal is an online payment system that allows you to send and receive money around the world. You can link your bank account, credit card, or debit card. You can also set up a PayPal Balance account.

Here’s how you can sign up on the app:

  • Tap Sign Up
  • Enter your email, phone number, and personal info.
  • Add one or multiple payment methods to your PayPal wallet. You can link your debit card, credit card, and bank account.

PayPal works to protect your financial data, payments, and eligible purchases every step of the way. Every transaction is encrypted to ensure your information remains private. We don't share your financial details with sellers, and our secure login options, alongside 24/7 fraud monitoring, keep your account protected against unauthorized access. With early fraud detection and real-time app alerts, we're proactive about your safety. Plus, PayPal Purchase Protection offers peace of mind by covering eligible purchases, helping to ensure you’re reimbursed for the purchase price plus original shipping if orders are not received or as described.

PayPal offers fast, secure online payments around the world. It's free to sign up and use our app for sending and receiving money. With PayPal, you get cash back on purchases from a wide range of brands, plus bonus cash back offers for using PayPal frequently. The PayPal Cashback Mastercard® allows you to earn unlimited cash back on all purchases.4 Additionally, you can combine offers to save, and roll your cash back into a high yield PayPal Savings account to earn a current 4.30% APY.5

With PayPal you can: send and request money, shop in-store or online, manage bank and card info, get cash back offers, discounts (where available), and flexible payment options.

Please note PayPal services may vary from country to country. You can find the full list of countries where PayPal offers services here. Click your country/region’s flag to see what services we offer.

PayPal offers a wide array of payment options for flexibility and convenience. You can use your bank account or popular debit and credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, Discover, and American Express. This selection encompasses options like the PayPal Debit Mastercard and PayPal Cashback Mastercard®. If you hold a PayPal Balance account, it's another payment method at your disposal. Additionally, PayPal Pay Later provides an option to split your payments, catering to various financial preferences and needs.
